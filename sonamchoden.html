Hot air balloon is a lighter than air aircraft consisting of a bag called an envelope which contains heated air. The hot air balloon is the first successful human carrying fight teachnology. Hot air balloon is a magical adventure

Safety tips when riding a hot air balloon

It is important to follow safety regulation when boarding and riding a hot air.

1-Wear sporty clothing

For the durtion of a hot air balloon ride, you will be standing so make sure you wear comfortable clothing and shoes.

2-No ballooning in bad weather

Hot air balloons are a fair-weather activity. If there is wind or rain, we will reschedule your tour to make sure your hot air balloon ride is safe.

3-Only people in good physical condition can go ballooning

In order to board a hot air balloon, you must be over 4 feet tall, over 6 years old, not pregnant and have no lingering illnesses or ailments that my make it difficult to be at high altitude or to stand for a long period of time.

The difference between hot air balloon and parachutes

Hot Air BalloonParachutes
Hot air balloons are able to rise and fall thanks to hot air. Hot air is less dense than cold air so it rises and carries the hot air balloon up with it. This is because of buoyancy. Parachutes can only be used to slow someone's decent. They play on air resistance or drag to slow down someone's fall. They cannot be used to go back up again.
